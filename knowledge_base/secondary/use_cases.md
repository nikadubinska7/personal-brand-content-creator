# AI Use Cases in Supply Chain and Business Operations

## Purpose of This File

This file lists practical AI and automation use cases that can be used as content inspiration.

The use cases should be framed as ideas, prototypes, or practical opportunities, not as guaranteed results.

## Use Case 1: Demand Forecasting Support

### Business Context

Demand planning requires understanding many variables, such as sales history, promotions, inventory, market activity, seasonality, price changes, and external signals.

### AI Opportunity

AI can support teams by:

- Detecting unusual demand changes
- Explaining possible demand drivers
- Flagging forecast risks
- Suggesting areas for review
- Supporting what-if scenarios
- Connecting forecast changes to inventory risk

### Content Angle

Forecasting AI is not only about prediction. It becomes more useful when it helps teams understand what changed, why it changed, and what action may be needed.

## Use Case 2: Forecast Validation

### Business Context

Forecasts may be submitted before products are available, after key cut-off dates, or without enough supporting context.

### AI / Automation Opportunity

Automation can support teams by:

- Checking forecast timing
- Comparing forecast dates with product availability
- Identifying mismatches
- Preparing follow-up messages
- Standardizing review logic

### Content Angle

A simple validation workflow can prevent operational issues before they become larger planning problems.

## Use Case 3: Supplier Follow-Up Assistant

### Business Context

Supplier communication often includes repeated follow-ups, missing confirmations, and scattered status updates.

### AI Opportunity

AI can support by:

- Drafting follow-up messages
- Summarizing open issues
- Prioritizing urgent supplier responses
- Preparing escalation notes
- Tracking repeated delays

### Content Angle

AI can reduce communication friction when the workflow and expected inputs are clear.

## Use Case 4: Logistics Exception Summary

### Business Context

Logistics teams need to respond quickly to shipment delays, stockout risks, and delivery exceptions.

### AI Opportunity

AI can support by:

- Summarizing exception cases
- Grouping issues by severity
- Drafting customer or internal updates
- Suggesting next actions
- Preparing daily exception reports

### Content Angle

AI is useful when it helps teams focus attention on the exceptions that matter most.

## Use Case 5: Monthly KPI Reporting Assistant

### Business Context

Operations teams often spend time preparing recurring reports and explaining performance changes.

### AI Opportunity

AI can support by:

- Summarizing KPI movement
- Explaining possible causes
- Highlighting anomalies
- Drafting report commentary
- Preparing management updates

### Content Angle

The value is not only saving time. The value is faster understanding of what changed and where attention is needed.

## Use Case 6: Inventory Risk Detection

### Business Context

Inventory decisions must balance stock availability, working capital, forecast accuracy, and service levels.

### AI Opportunity

AI can support by:

- Detecting potential stockouts
- Highlighting slow-moving stock
- Connecting inventory risk with demand signals
- Suggesting review priorities
- Supporting replenishment decisions

### Content Angle

AI can support inventory management by helping teams see risks earlier, but final decisions still need business context.

## Use Case 7: Document Summarization for Operations

### Business Context

Business teams work with contracts, supplier documents, meeting notes, policies, and reports.

### AI Opportunity

AI can support by:

- Summarizing documents
- Extracting action items
- Identifying missing information
- Preparing Q&A over internal documents
- Creating structured notes

### Content Angle

Document AI becomes useful when it reduces the time between information and action.

## Use Case 8: Internal Knowledge Assistant

### Business Context

Operational knowledge is often spread across documents, emails, spreadsheets, and people.

### AI Opportunity

A knowledge assistant can support by:

- Retrieving relevant internal context
- Answering process questions
- Summarizing policies
- Explaining standard workflows
- Helping new team members find information

### Content Angle

RAG is not only a technical architecture. In business terms, it is a way to make internal knowledge easier to use.

## Use Case 9: Sales and Operations Planning Support

### Business Context

S&OP requires coordination across sales, operations, finance, and supply chain.

### AI Opportunity

AI can support by:

- Summarizing inputs from different teams
- Highlighting conflicts between demand and supply
- Preparing meeting briefs
- Tracking decisions and actions
- Creating scenario summaries

### Content Angle

AI can support cross-functional planning when it improves visibility, structure, and follow-up.

## Use Case Selection Criteria

A good AI use case should have:

- A clear business process
- Repetitive or high-friction work
- Available data or documents
- A defined user
- A clear human review point
- A measurable business outcome
- A realistic prototype scope

## Use Cases to Avoid as First MVPs

Avoid starting with:

- Fully autonomous supply chain decisions
- Black-box forecasting with no explanation
- Large enterprise transformation programs
- Unclear productivity goals
- Processes with no owner
- Workflows with poor or unavailable data
- Automation where errors create high operational risk without review
