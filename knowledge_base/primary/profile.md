# Personal Profile

## Professional Positioning

I have a background in supply chain operations, logistics, marketplace operations, purchasing, and business process improvement.

I am currently developing expertise in AI consulting, AI integration, and business process automation, with a focus on applying AI to supply chain and operational workflows.

My content should show a practical understanding of business operations combined with growing AI implementation capability.

The positioning should not overclaim senior AI implementation expertise. It should show thoughtful analysis, experimentation, learning, and practical prototype-building.

## Current Direction

My professional direction is to connect supply chain operations experience with AI tools, AI agents, automation, and business process improvement.

I am especially interested in how AI can support the following to create tangible business impact:

- Supply chain operations
- Sales operations
- Forecasting and planning
- Reporting automation
- Logistics coordination
- Supplier communication
- Business process analysis
- Repetitive operational tasks
- Decision support

## Target Audience

My content is mainly for:

- Potential employers
- Potential future clients
- Supply chain leaders
- Operations managers
- Business owners exploring AI
- AI and automation professionals
- Consultants working with business transformation

## Personal Brand Position

I write about practical AI use in business and supply chain from the perspective of someone with real operational background who is actively building AI consulting and integration skills.

The content should demonstrate:

- Ability to understand business processes
- Ability to analyze where AI can create value
- Awareness of current AI trends in business
- Logical and structured thinking
- Practical judgment about when AI is useful and when it is not
- Ability to build prototype tools and test ideas
- Understanding of supply chain strategy and operations context

## Differentiation

My differentiation angle is:

- I connect AI to real supply chain and operations workflows.
- I connect operations with real business impact, creating the tools which focus not only on simplification and automation, but also on bringing the strategic business impact on the table.
- I analyze AI through business process logic.
- I focus on practical use cases.
- I show how AI can support operational and strategic decision-making.
- I explain limitations, risks, and implementation conditions.
- I use prototype projects to demonstrate applied learning.

## Main Content Themes

- Practical AI use cases in supply chain
- AI agents in business operations
- AI for logistics, forecasting, and reporting
- Why AI requires clear processes and good context
- How to evaluate AI opportunities in operations
- Lessons from building AI prototype tools
- How AI can support, not replace, operational teams
- how AI can streamline your business results
- Differences between AI hype and useful automation
